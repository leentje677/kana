KitsuKana Help 🦊

📚 Practising

  • Auto-Advance: A correct single kana answer moves forward after 1 second.
  • Word Mode: Vocabulary won't skip ahead — study the meaning, then press Next.
  • Numbers: For number words you can type the digit 7, the English seven, or romaji nana — all accepted.
  • Hint: Shows the first letter without breaking your streak.
  • Skip: Move to the next card without counting it wrong.
  • Slow: Replays audio at half speed for clearer pronunciation.
  • Audio: Tap the character any time to hear it again.

🗂️ Modes & Categories

  • Mode dropdown: Choose a kana group, vocabulary set, or a full combined test.
  • Topic dropdown: Appears for vocabulary modes — drill just Animals, Numbers, Colors, etc.
  • あ / ア button: Instantly switches between Test All Hiragana and Test All Katakana.
  • MC Mode: Switches from typing to four-choice tap buttons. Distractors come from the same topic.
  • Loop: Reshuffles the deck endlessly when a session ends.
  • Timer: Enables a 60-second countdown for speed practice.

📊 Stats

  • 🔥 Streak: Correct answers in a row — resets on any wrong answer.
  • 🏆 Best: Your longest streak ever.
  • 📅 Days: Consecutive days practiced — miss a day and it resets!
  • Accuracy: Your correct-answer rate across all sessions.
  • Tap any stat to see a short explanation.

🔥 Weak Words

  • Anything you answer wrong is saved automatically.
  • Tap Practice Weak Words to drill only those.

🧩 Break Time

  • Scroll to the bottom and tap Need a break? to open the Akari light puzzle — a fun way to rest your brain between sessions.
