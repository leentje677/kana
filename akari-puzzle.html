Akari Help 🏮

  • Place a bulb: Tap any empty cell. Tap again to remove it.
  • Mark a cell: Long-press (mobile) or right-click (desktop) to mark a cell as bulb-free. It won't count against you — it's just scratch paper.
  • Numbered walls: A wall with a number needs exactly that many bulbs in the cells directly touching it.
  • Beams: Bulbs light their whole row and column until blocked by a wall. Two bulbs can never shine on each other.
  • Hint: Reveals one safe bulb placement, prioritizing logical deductions.
  • Win condition: Every empty cell is lit, every wall number is exactly satisfied, and no beams cross.
